Meet the Company

The concept for Arbor Automation was created by manufacturing innovators while designing new parts and components. After creating CAD (computer aided design) layouts for new parts they began the cumbersome process of moving into production via existing CAM (computer aided manufacturing) software and thought “there has to be a better way.” An initial proof of concept showed promise that the human led CAM layout process, which currently takes hours, could be “automated” and completed in minutes.

Arbor Automation’s purpose is to keep engineers and technical designers focused on creation and innovation by significantly accelerating and streamlining the CAM process via advanced software and processing engines.
